Location

Located 250 km north of Delhi in the state of Punjab

About

Weather

Mohali experiences four distinct seasons. Summers can be hot with temperatures over 40°C, monsoons bring relief with rainfall, autumns are mild, and winters can be cold with temperatures dropping to 1°C.

Population

Mohali has a diverse population with a mix of cultures and traditions. As of the latest census, the population of Mohali is estimated to be around 1.6 million (1.6 million) people.

Sports

Mohali is known for its sports culture and is home to the PCA Stadium, hosting various cricket matches and events.

Things To Do

  • Visit historic landmarks like the Rose Garden and Fateh Burj.
  • Explore beautiful parks and nature reserves, such as Shalimar Park.
  • Try out the local cuisine, including Punjabi dishes like Sarson da Saag and Makki di Roti.
